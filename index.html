About Me

I've always been interested in the human mind. Why do people do the things they do? It's what led me towards marketing in the first place. At its core, marketing has always been about understanding people; whether its understanding how to draw attention on social media, understanding how to build relationships with a brand's community, or even crunching numbers to explain consumers' thoughts through the use of analytics.

I've worked as a marketing consultant in a number of different industries including health & fitness, data storage, agriculture, healthcare, and fashion. My experience is centered on social media management/analytics, data analytics, email marketing, and copyright. I'm passionate about applying my creativity to product experiences, visual design, and data analysis.
